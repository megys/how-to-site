How to Make Ratatouille

Ever wanted to eat that delicious delicacy from the Pixar's classic Ratatouille? Well now you can!

(which is surprisingly, not pepperoni)

What You'll Need

  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 yellow squash
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

What To Do

  1. Blanch the tomatoes, and cut 4 into small slices. Set aside.
  2. Slice the zucchinis and squash with a mandolin. Set aside. (if you don't have a mandolin, just cut them into small slices)
  3. Dice the remaining 2 tomatoes, the bell pepper, the onion, and the garlic.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. SautÃ© the onion, garlic, and bell peppers until soft, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, then add the tomatoes.
  5. Place mixture into a blender with some more olive oil. Add rosemary. Blend until slightly chunky.
  6. In a round baking dish, spread the blended mixture over the dish.
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  8. Arrange the sliced veggies in alternating patterns, on top of the sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Cover the pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover, then bake for another 20 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.
  10. Enjoy! (it tastes really good with some baguette bread)
